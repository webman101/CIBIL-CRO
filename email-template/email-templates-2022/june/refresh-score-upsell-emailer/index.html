When should you check your CIBIL Score & Report?

If you are planning to take a loan

Get your CIBIL Report 6 months prior to a loan application and clear your discrepancies for faster approval.

If you have been denied credit

Review your CIBIL Report to see why you were denied credit. You can dispute any inaccuracies and reapply for a loan on resolution.

If you suspect your identity has been stolen

You can eliminate chances of loan denial on the basis of identity theft by accessing your CIBIL Report regularly.

If you are planning to improve your credit profile

Be financially fit. Plan your finances by using your CIBIL Report to create a plan for achieving your financial goals.
